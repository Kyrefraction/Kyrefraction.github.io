Employment experience

AutoConvert

Software developer (2018-present)

I began work at AutoConvert in September 2018. Since then I have become experienced team member, partcipating and leading sprint meetings and daily stand-ups. As a full-stack developer I've been exposed to a multitude of different tasks and technologies. I worked on creating integrations with lenders like BlackHorse, FCA and Vauxhall in both XML and JSON.
